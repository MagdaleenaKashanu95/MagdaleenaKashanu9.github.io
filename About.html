ABOUT US

Covid Response Team is a team put together by the Ministry of Health to Educate Namibians about Covid-19 and give daily updates about where we stand in terms of COVID-19. It consists of proffessionals from different departments working together to curb the spread of corona virus. It provides updates on all our platforms.
